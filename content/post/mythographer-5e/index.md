Typst template for typsetting documents in the style of the fifth edition of Dungeon&Dragons.
This package has been heavily inspired by the awesome work done by the _rpgTex_ team on their [DND-5e-LaTeX-Template](https://github.com/rpgtex/DND-5e-LaTeX-Template).

It supports 99% of D&D's official manual features, automatic translations and it's highly customizable.
